Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects take 2-4 weeks from start to finish, depending on complexity and content requirements. We'll provide a detailed timeline in our proposal.

What information do you need to get started?

We'll need details about your business, target audience, goals, and any specific features or design preferences. We'll guide you through everything during our discovery call.

Do you provide hosting and maintenance?

Yes, we can recommend hosting solutions and provide ongoing maintenance packages. Our static websites are easy to host and maintain with minimal costs.

Can you help with content and copywriting?

We can help refine your content or connect you with professional copywriters who understand your industry and can create compelling website copy.

What if I need changes after the website is live?

We provide post-launch support and can make updates as needed. Our websites are built to be easily maintainable, and we offer ongoing support packages for regular updates.
